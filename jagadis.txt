Bose is a lunar impact crater that is located on the far side of the Moon, in the southern sphere hemisphere. It lies just to the northwest of the smaller crater Bhabha, and southeast of Alder. The crater is named after an eminent Indian polymath, Sir Jagadish Chandra Bose, for his works on wireless communication.
